Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 20, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Tik. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our service. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, including:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information.
  • The TikTok URLs you submit for download.
  • Your IP address or device information (beyond standard server logs for analytics).

Server Logs

Like most websites, our server automatically logs basic, anonymous information about visits. This includes the type of browser, time of visit, and pages requested. This data is used for website administration and to analyze trends, but it is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Cookies

We do not use cookies to track users or store personal information. Any cookies used are for essential site functionality only.

Third-Party Services

Our service acts as an intermediary to fetch video information from a third-party API. We do not control the privacy practices of TikTok or any other third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
